## Fact-check disagreements / resolutions

| Date | Claim | Disagreement | Resolution + source |
|---|---|---|---|
| 2026-04-26 | Lifetime Globalist requires "1,000 nights + 10 years at Globalist" | Copilot flagged this as an outdated myth | Corrected to **1,000,000 lifetime Base Points**; the 10-year requirement was dropped. Nights stayed do NOT count toward the threshold. Sources: [TPG](https://thepointsguy.com/loyalty-programs/world-of-hyatt-lifetime-elite-status/), [OMAAT](https://onemileatatime.com/guides/hyatt-lifetime-globalist-status/) |
| 2026-04-26 | Suite Upgrade Awards (4/yr), Club Access Awards (2/yr), Guest of Honor (2/yr) listed as Globalist tier benefits | Copilot flagged these as Milestone Rewards, not tier benefits | Restructured: Milestone Rewards is a separate system earned every 10 nights regardless of status. Effective Jan 1, 2024, Guest of Honor moved from Globalist tier benefit to Milestone Reward. Sources: [AwardWallet](https://awardwallet.com/hotels/world-of-hyatt/milestone-rewards/), [On Points With Kids](https://onpointswithkids.com/2026/03/hyatt-milestone-rewards-in-2026/) |
| 2026-04-26 | "One combine per 30 days" rule on points sharing | Copilot flagged as outdated framing | Softened — Hyatt rolling out **Digital Points Sharing in 2026** (replacing paper/PDF process). Old limits may not be the whole story going forward. Source: [OMAAT](https://onemileatatime.com/news/world-of-hyatt-free-points-sharing-early-award-night-access/) |
| 2026-04-26 | 5-tier chart described in neutral terms | Copilot pushed for "dynamic pricing in disguise" framing | Adopted — Hyatt avoids the "dynamic pricing" PR hit but functionally that's what wider tiered bands accomplish. Cat 8 Top hits 75K (up from 45K peak). Source: [Frequent Miler](https://frequentmiler.com/world-of-hyatt-announces-2026-category-changes-theyre-not-pretty/) |
| 2026-04-26 | Globalist free parking on award stays not initially listed | Copilot flagged as a meaningful omission | Added to Globalist tier benefits and Tips & quirks. Caveat: only applies when parking is billed to the room folio. Source: [TPG](https://thepointsguy.com/loyalty-programs/globalist-free-parking/) |
